As a kid, I have always believed in what I have lost was never mine but as I grew up, I added a new line to the saying- What I have gained was always mine. Blogging is one of those things that I feel I have gained and am probably going to treasure for the rest of my life.

**Who is this optimistic bossman?**

I have always wanted to start my own blog page and write what I feel about certain things around me. I am so glad my professor (Prof. Joanne Tombrakos) pushed us to start blogging and build this healthy habit!
